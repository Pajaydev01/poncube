SHIPPING AND CUSTOM INFORMATION

  What are the Shipping Charges and time?

The shipping charges vary with each seller and the state from which the product is being shipped. 

Sellers incurred high shipping charges on low valued items therefore in such situations; nominal delivery charges help them to set their offset logistics costs. In certain situations, the seller may waive off the shipping and delivery charges, if you choose to shop for a minimum predefined value.

For example, you may be required to pay 40 naira as delivery charges, if the product is less than 500 naira, however, the delivery charges may be waived off if the total cost is more than 500 naira.

You can get more information on shipping time on the product detail page.

What are the supported shipping methods?

Suppliers can easily choose from a large number of express shipping options; however since the shipping methods are set up by different suppliers, you can easily contact the supplier to get detailed shipping information. To get in touch with the supplier, visit the product detail page and click contact now to send in a message to the supplier.

Do the site charges custom and import duty?

If you are ordering items from Poncube to your own country, the order may be required to clear custom in the country. Therefore, while you order, please check whether or not the order will incur additional import duty or other custom related charges.
If the order is stuck with custom clearance in your country, the supplier will not be responsible for the delay. However, here we hope that as a customer, you will not use additional import costs or custom clearance delay as a reason to raise a claim or leave a negative feedback.

  BEFORE THE ORDER HAS BEEN SHIPPED

Can the processing time be extended?

Yes, the processing time can be extended. If you agree to extend the processing time for the supplier, follow the below-written steps:

  • Sign in to your order and locate your current order from the list

  • Click on the “extend the processing time” button

  • Choose a time slot you want your order to be extended and click the “confirm button”

Managing Shipping Address

Since the language of our website is English, we request you to fill in all the details in English input method. If there are certain terms that are not in English, you are advised to use similar English language.

Moreover, to make sure you get your order on time, confirm your shipping address. Click on my order page and select the shipping option. Here you can recheck your address and click save.


  AFTER SHIPPING OF THE ORDER

How many days will it take to ship my order?

Generally, the order is delivered within 3 to 60 days depending on your delivery location. However, an extension on the date of delivery can be made if the seller and buyer mutually agree on a new date.

However, if the seller has not shipped the order before the agreed date, the buyer can contact the supplier to agree on a new date or ask for a refund.

How can I track my order?

After your order has been shipped, a tracking number will appear. The order can easily be tracked with the number provided. Log in to www.poncube.com and see the tracking details in transactions.

Note : It may take up to 48 hours for the tracking information to appear and get updated by the shipping company. However, if after 3 days the tracking information is not updated, contact the seller.
