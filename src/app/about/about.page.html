ABOUT PONCUBE

Our Story

It all started with a thought

To go beyond limitations

Work beyond geographical boundaries

Work for the betterment of local artists

Make a difference to the country’s economy


The Poncube.com was founded in 2017 with a vision to promote local artists in Nigeria and give them a platform to showcase their work to the world.

Raised in the city of Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria, Founder Moruf Olansile Olalere always wanted to give back to the community. Coming from a business family, with both the parents being successful entrepreneurs, entrepreneurship blood runs in the family. Moruf studied business administration at Cyprus International University and shifted his base to the USA to pursue his career. However, after living in the USA, he realized his urge to give back something to the community and help people living in Nigeria achieve a better lifestyle.


Therefore, he designed poncube.com to enable local sellers to grow their reach beyond the country and sell their goods to people all over the world. He has seen the rise of the music industry as one of the most successful industries in Nigeria in the past decade and this motivated him even further to patronize his country.


Our Vision

To offer every crafts person a chance to reach customers within and beyond Nigeria.

We want to help small businesses, exporters and manufacturers in Nigeria sell their products around the globe and contribute towards the country’s economy. At Poncube, we reinvent the way people are used to selling their products. We rethink the selling process and spend hours giving the right platform to sellers and buyers to interact.

We have seen the growth of music industry in the past decade due to Nigerians patronizing their artists and supporting them. Therefore, we envision extending our support to fellow Nigerian artist who have the talent and potential to come together for the growth of the country’s economy. We can easily achieve what industries in China and other countries have achieved in a matter of few years.

Our Philosophy

We are constantly asking ourselves, “How can we help improve the country’s economy? How can we promote local artists?” We connect millions of buyers around the globe with Nigerian sellers, thus empowering them and creating great opportunities. In the coming few years, we want to become one of the best destination for discovering great economic opportunities.

Our Promise

  • We offer a platform where people come to sell, buy and give.

  • We promote talent that comes in every shape, size, and age.

  • We continue to innovate and come forward with great opportunities.

  • From listing to payments and delivery, everything is secure.

  • We will stay true to our customers and serve the finest quality Nigerian goods.
